This page contains all the resources relevant for introductory quantitative finance course. Resources are added as and when they are relevant for the topics covered in the course.

Official Resources: These are the material and textbooks recommended by university of london.

  • Elements of Financial Risk Management, Peter.F.Chritofferssen; Elements of Forecasting, Diebold .
  • Study Guide (2015)

Classic Textbook: Quantitative Risk Management - Concepts, Techniques and Tools by Alexander J. McNeil, Rudiger Frey, Paul Embrechts. Its a classic with very concise and comprehensive introduction to the subject of quantitative risk management. Please find it here.

Online Courses: These are some of the courses offered at other universities which cover the similar material. All the material including video lectures are usually available in these course websites.

  • Topics in Mathematics with Applications in Finance, 2013, MIT
  • Financial Markets(2011), Yale [for a good introduction to financial markets]
  • Financial Theory, Yale [for a solid introduction to financial theory which underlies financial markets]
